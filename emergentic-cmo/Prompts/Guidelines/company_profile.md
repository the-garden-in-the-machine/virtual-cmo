# Emergentic - Company Profile

## Core Business
Emergentic builds creative automation systems, custom media pipelines, and software-driven content production environments for modern businesses. We combine automation engineering with design pipelines to solve bottleneck issues in content delivery, social media publishing, and business operations.

## Service Offerings & Tech Stack
1. **Creative Automation Pipelines:** End-to-end pipelines that convert raw content assets, narration, and subtitles directly into optimized mp4 output files using integrations like Apple `osascript`, Adobe `ExtendScript`, and custom CLI tools.
2. **Web Scrapers & Data Engines:** Customized web crawlers and lead generators built using Playwright/Stealth to gather, prioritize, and structure prospect data.
3. **Agentic System Integrations:** Implementing frameworks (like Hermes) to run workflows and automate repetitious tasks under human supervision.

## Target Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
Our focus is on creative and lifestyle brands based in Los Angeles that are content-heavy but engineering-light:
* **Art & Design Agencies / Design Studios:** Companies executing high volumes of content but looking to scale production workflows (e.g., Collins, Pentagram, Order).
* **Fashion & Lifestyle Brands:** Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) brands that rely on continuous publishing, visual marketing, and multi-channel posting (e.g., Reformation, Cuyana, Vuori).
* **Film & Video Production Houses:** Studios managing footage, subtitles, transcribing, and distribution assets (e.g., Smuggler, PRETTYBIRD).
* **Entertainment & Podcasting Networks:** Content houses looking to automate audiogram rendering, post-formatting, and episode distribution pipelines (e.g., Wondery, Wondery, Crooked Media).

## Key Value Propositions
* **Time & Cost Efficiency:** Automating high-friction, repetitive tasks (like video resizing, overlay rendering, and subtitle burns) to let creative teams focus on storytelling.
* **Process Reliability:** Building robust, sandboxed execution flows that remove human error from formatting and publishing schedules.
* **Custom Fit:** Engineering bespoke tools that wrap around the client's existing workflow and design guidelines, rather than forcing them into generic software packages.
