Heuristic Framework Specification

Prioritization Heuristics

Score = (M × 3.0) + (R × 2.0) + (A × 1.5) + (B × 1.0)
M

Mutual Contacts (Weight: 3.0)

Gauges Warm path availability. Shared network contacts reduce trust friction and elevate LinkedIn message response rates.

R

Title Seniority (Weight: 2.0)

Identifies decision-making and contract signing authority (Heads of Production, CBDOs, and Founders rate highly).

A

AI-Alignment Affinity (Weight: 1.5)

Assesses willingness to integrate automated engines. High-volume design studios rate highly; traditional studios score lower.

B

Content Scale Need (Weight: 1.0)

Measures visual/video rendering volume bottlenecks. High publishing volumes create direct automation needs.
