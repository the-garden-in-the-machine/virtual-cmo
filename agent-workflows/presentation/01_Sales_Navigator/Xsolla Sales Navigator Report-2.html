01

Initialization & Context Loading

orchestrator · .env

Loads environment settings and validates the GEMINI_API_KEY and APOLLO_API_KEY, prompting via CLI if missing. Parses target company profiles and outreach guidelines to set the AI writer's tone and constraints.

02

Company-First Browser Scraping

sales_nav_adapter.js

Loads a pre-captured auth.json session to bypass bot detection and 2FA. Resolves the company name to its Sales Navigator ID (875431 for Xsolla), extracts department headcount trends over 3-month and 1-year windows, and maps the executive buying committee from the "Key People" panel — emulating human clicks to capture tenure, screenshots, and WebM recordings.

03

Apollo Match API Enrichment

enrichWithApollo

Sends a POST to api.apollo.io/v1/people/match authenticated via the X-Api-Key header. Splits names into first_name / last_name and matches on company, domain, and title.

Resolves corporate emails, with reveal_personal_emails: true as a fallback when business matches are restricted.

04

Batch LLM Processing

evaluateProspectsBatch

Combines each prospect's profile with the company's growth trends (e.g. Engineering +29% in 12 months) and has Gemini write a quiet, hyper-personalized draft — avoiding high-hype language in favor of low-friction, natural conversation.

05

Exports & Standalone Packaging

package_prospector.py

Writes structured prospects to scraped_leads.csv, creates individual Markdown outreach drafts, and runs a packaging routine that rebuilds a clean standalone folder into emergentic.zip on the Desktop with localized paths.

04

Protocol Recommendations

Three features to scale these manual insights across the automated prospecting campaign.

1

Pre-scrape company diagnostics

Before pulling individual leads, navigate to the target company page and inspect department headcount metrics. Dynamic pitch tuning: if Engineering is growing >15%, pivot outreach to product/technical collaboration; if Marketing leads, pivot to branding and advertising.

2

Auto-extract buying committees

Rather than complex lead searches that miss non-standard titles, parse names directly from the "Key People" panel of the company profile — mapping core decision-makers in under two seconds.

3

Integrated contact enrichment

Using the verified X-Api-Key header format, batch-query api.apollo.io/v1/people/match post-scrape to resolve emails, bypass manual searches, and output a complete campaign-ready spreadsheet.
