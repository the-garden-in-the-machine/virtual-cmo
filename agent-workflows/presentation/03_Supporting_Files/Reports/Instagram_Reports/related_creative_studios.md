## 🏛️ Experiential & Large-Scale Immersive Collectives

### 1. `@teamlab` (teamLab)
* **Website:** [teamlab.art](https://www.teamlab.art/)
* **Focus:** International digital art collective producing massive, permanent interactive projection museums (e.g., teamLab Borderless).
* **B2B Synergy:** High-volume spatial projection mappings, real-time sensory tracking, and custom rendering engines.

### 2. `@momentfactory` (Moment Factory)
* **Website:** [momentfactory.com](https://momentfactory.com/)
* **Focus:** Montreal-based multimedia studio specializing in large-scale interactive public light shows, concerts, and architectural projection.
* **B2B Synergy:** Custom control software integration, video playback automation, and large-scale experiential storytelling.

### 3. `@ouuch_studio` (Ouchhh Studio)
* **Website:** [ouchhh.tv](https://ouchhh.tv/)
* **Focus:** Creative agency using machine learning, neural networks, and astronomical data to generate immersive visual structures and "AI data paintings."
* **B2B Synergy:** Generative AI aesthetics, custom data-visualization pipelines, and projection mapping.

### 4. `@refikanadol` (Refik Anadol Studio)
* **Website:** [refikanadol.com](https://refikanadol.com/)
* **Focus:** Studio pioneering data aesthetics and machine learning visuals, projecting fluid AI hallucinations onto iconic architecture.
* **B2B Synergy:** High-performance model training pipelines, real-time generative GPU environments, and custom spatial projection.

### 5. `@superblueart` (Superblue)
* **Website:** [superblue.com](https://www.superblue.com/)
* **Focus:** Experiential art centers presenting large-scale installations (light, sound, kinetics) in repurposed industrial facilities.
* **B2B Synergy:** Art-science production networks, sensory hardware control systems, and exhibition layout systems.

### 6. `@unitedvisualartists` (UVA)
* **Website:** [uva.co.uk](https://www.uva.co.uk/)
* **Focus:** Studio integrating physical sculptures with responsive light, kinetic motion, and spatial sound.
* **B2B Synergy:** Custom kinetics software, DMX controller automation, and responsive real-time graphics pipelines.

* **B2B Synergy:** Custom workflow tools, node-based automation assets, and digital art generation pipelines.

### 8. `@recraft.ai` (Recraft)
* **Website:** [recraft.ai](https://www.recraft.ai/)

### 10. `@man.vs.machine` (ManvsMachine)
* **Website:** [manvsmachine.co.uk](https://www.manvsmachine.co.uk/)
* **Focus:** Creative studio producing high-end design, motion graphics, and automated visual content campaigns for tech and apparel brands.
* **B2B Synergy:** 3D rendering pipeline optimization, Houdini script automation, and programmatic video composing.
