## 📌 Executive Summary & Key Alignments

* **Strategic Pivot:** Shifted focus from gaming NPCs to **virtual event spaces** (e.g., event registration systems, community-focused Metaverse applications). The primary value proposition is helping enterprise networking by simulating events before they occur and generating targeted conversation starters.
* **Lead Generation & Tooling:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator chosen as the primary lead generation tool. Access credentials for the Sales Navigator account have been resolved.
* **Automated Browser Outreach:** Because LinkedIn lacks an official API, the team will implement automated browser interactions (e.g., using **Playwright** or **Selenium**).
* **Social Media Scope Refocus:** Shifting away from competitor style research/analysis to directly creating content narratives, visual framing, and post proposals. This will assist the design team in bootstrapping visual assets.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Privacy & Compliance Warning:** When targeting art technology firms with simulated community data, the team must prioritize navigating privacy compliance and data safety carefully, as simulated customer/community data can be sensitive and controversial.
